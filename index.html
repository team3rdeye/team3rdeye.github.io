Current state

India has only 1% of the world's vehicles but 11% of the global deaths from road accidents occur in India, according to a report by the World Bank released earlier this year.

There are numerous reasons for these car accidents, but all of them boil down to the negligence of the driver. Drivers are often busy on their phones whilst driving. They could be on calls, texting, checking the GPS etc.

We are here to help

Our product "The Third Eye" is an alarm device that will be placed at the front of any automobile.

Under the circumstance in which the driver doesn't pay attention and something comes in front of the car, this device would start beeping and alarm the driver and the driver could act in time, slowing or stopping the car.

This will not only ensure safety of the driver but also that of the pedestrian or a stray animal or another car etc.

Finances

According to estimates the growing international road safety market is expected to reach rupees 46150 lakh crores (5.8 billion dollars) by 2026.

We aim to make the device affordable and accessible to all, and thus the cost price of 1 device will be approximately 400 rupees. We will be selling it for 800 rupees.

We have mapped out and planned in detail our sources of funds, that include invoice financing, ACH business loans and debt & equity financing.
